Who We Are

We’re 600+ residents—families, seniors, and essential workers—working together to ensure decisions about our homes are transparent, lawful, and community‑centered. Changes since 2017 (e.g., easement amendments and zoning lot mergers) enabled large‑scale construction that we believe conflicts with our bylaws and community interests.

We are seeking accountability, environmental safeguards, and a fair process. Support our effort by donating and sharing verified documents below.
